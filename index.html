About
Poonam Mishra

I specialize in group and one-on-one yoga sessions tailored for beginners, seniors, and people dealing with stiffness, back pain, and lifestyle-related issues.

With a deep understanding of how the body and mind work together, I design each session to suit your unique needs — whether you're just beginning your yoga journey or looking to deepen your practice.
